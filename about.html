I am an enthusiast for web designing and UI/UX designing living in Cluj-Napoca and having a degree in Computer Science. I graduated from Babes Bolyai University in 2016.

My passion for UI/UX designing resides in using Photoshop from a young age for photo and text editing and later on for designing my own concept websites. After a while from discovering Photoshop I also started to use occasionally Illustrator and Fireworks.

In high school I also started to develop a test for web designing. That lead me to seek inspiration all over the internet. I often used to surf the internet for websites only to see what components (what images, shapes, colors, fonts or even animations were used for a certain situation) are in trend. This urge of seeking inspiration didn’t leave me till this day.Moreover, I begun learning HTML, CSS so I can implement my designs.

I like to think of myself, when it comes to UI/UX designing, as a person with an inclination for the websites with flat and minimalist styles, a person who always thought that “less is more”.
